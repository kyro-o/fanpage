About the Show

Happy Camp or 快乐大本营 is one of China's most popular shows, consistently acheiving record high ratings and viewership with a minimum active viewership of tens of millions. The show features celebrities and famous figures from all over the world, including musicians, actors, athletes, and idols.

The Happy Family: He Jiong, Li Weijia, Xie Na, Du Haitao, and Wu Xin, hosts the show. Guests and hosts of the show participates in various activies. These activities include performances, games, interviews, parodies, and more.

Due to its tremendous popularity and success, Happy Camp has won numerous awards across Asia, been recognized internationally. This show provides a stage for celebrities to showcase their works and productions, including movies, books, and music. Over the years, the Happy Camp Family has produced numerous music albums and movies.
